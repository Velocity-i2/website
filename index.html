Our Architectural Roadmap

We provide a reliable, structured path from initial schematic diagrams to final mass production, ensuring high yield rates and rock-solid field performance.

PHASE 01

Requirements & Architecture

We analyze your concept, define key components, map signal paths, perform feasibility calculations, and establish regulatory compliance targets from day one.

PHASE 02

Schematic & PCB Layout

We capture schematics and lay out multi-layer PCBs with advanced signal integrity, power distribution network routing, thermal modeling, and structural DFM validation.

PHASE 03

Prototyping & Mass Prep

We assemble functional prototypes, implement bootloaders/firmware, run validation testing, and assist you with manufacturing partners for rapid, certified production scaling.
