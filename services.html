Capabilities

Core Engineering Services

We deliver complete full-stack electronics product engineering, providing our global clients with a singular point of accountability from schematics to high-volume shipping.

High-Speed & RF PCB Design

Specialized

Laying out complex, multi-layer high-frequency circuit boards. We manage strict physical and electromagnetic rulesets, impedance targets, and signal trace routing bounds.

  • Up to 32 Layers High-Speed Impedance Control
  • Blind & Buried Microvia stackups, Rigid-Flex Layouts
  • Signal Integrity (SI) & Power Integrity (PI) Simulations
View PCB Layout Details

Embedded System Design

Our hardware engineering team defines robust component architectures, maps power distribution networks, and designs full schematics with a focus on reliability.

  • Microcontroller & SoC Selection (ARM Cortex, RISC-V, ESP32)
  • Precision Analog Front-End (AFE) Signal Conditioning
  • Low-Power Rail Optimization & LDO/Buck Cascades

Firmware Development

We write elegant, highly-optimized C/C++ firmware to make your hardware perform reliably. Our code runs bare-metal or on Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS).

  • Real-Time Operating Systems (FreeRTOS, Zephyr RTOS)
  • Custom Driver Implementation (SPI, I2C, UART, CAN, USB)
  • Sleep State Optimization & Ultra-Low Standby Current

IoT Solutions & Connected Devices

We deploy robust edge communication capabilities, connecting low-power remote sensors securely to public or private cloud telemetry architectures.

  • Cellular IoT (LTE-M, NB-IoT), Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.4, LoRaWAN
  • Secure Cloud Integration (MQTT, WebSockets, HTTPS to AWS/Azure)
  • Secure Bootloaders & Firmware Over-the-Air (FOTA) Updates

End-to-End Product Development

We provide comprehensive concept-to-market product engineering, coordinating hardware, software, casing layout, and system validation.

  • Enclosure Fitting & Mechanical Layout Coordination (3D CAD)
  • Ingress Protection (IP65/IP67) thermal and stress planning
  • Bill of Materials (BOM) cost optimization & sourcing plans

Prototype Manufacturing Support

We eliminate friction when stepping up to initial bench prototypes, validating schematics and debugging hardware systems in our laboratory.

  • Desktop Assembly and Quick-Turn Board Sourcing
  • Hardware Debugging & Signal Oscilloscope Verification
  • Lab testing of power ripples, clock skew, and logic states

Reverse Engineering

We perform systematic teardowns, extract schematics from legacy multi-layer PCBs, upgrade obsolete parts, and enhance existing designs.

  • PCB Layer Delamination & Trace Extraction
  • Obsolescence redesign with direct drop-in modern alternatives
  • Schematic reconstruction & feature additions

Industrial Automation

We design custom rugged electronic boards capable of withstanding harsh, high-vibration, high-EMI, and extreme temperature industrial fields.

  • High Galvanic Isolation (Optocouplers, Magnetic Isolators)
  • Industrial Protocol Interfaces (RS485 Modbus, CAN Bus, EtherCAT)
  • Transient Voltage Suppression (TVS), Surge & Reverse Polarity Safety

Custom Electronics

Tailor-made hardware modules built for unique specifications: custom sensor interfaces, power drivers, and proprietary communication bridges.

  • Specialized transducer and sensor signal conditioning
  • High-efficiency motor control & power driver boards
  • Bespoke telemetry nodes and localized control loops

Production Support & EMS Hand-off

We compile complete production-ready data packages, ensuring smooth transitions to high-volume contract manufacturing.

  • Design for Manufacturability (DFM) & Testing (DFT) audits
  • Standard production packs (Gerber, Drill, ODB++, XY Data, BOM)
  • Direct liaison with Electronic Manufacturing Services (EMS) partners

Have a custom engineering challenge?

Our principal system architects will review your schematic outline, draft high-level guidelines, and provide a detailed structural engineering estimate.
