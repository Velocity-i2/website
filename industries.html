Sectors Served

Target Market Verticals

We engineer tailored hardware stacks meeting the precise compliance, reliability, and security regulations of diverse tech sectors.

Automotive & EV Tech

We design electronic subsystems for electric vehicles, focusing on Battery Management Systems (BMS), high-accuracy battery balancing, and isolated CAN/LIN ECU controllers.

STANDARDS: ISO 26262 ASIL-D, AEC-Q100

Medical Electronics

We lay out certified hardware boards for medical monitoring systems, diagnostic ventilators, and wearable health sensors with stringent electrical safety guardrails.

STANDARDS: IEC 60601-1, ISO 13485 Certified

Smart Energy & Power Grids

We develop smart meters, electrical solar monitoring inverters, sub-station telemetry nodes, and heavy energy controllers with high surge and isolation tolerances.

STANDARDS: IEC 61850 Grid Compliance

Industrial IoT (IIoT)

Deploying robust sensor gateways and isolated remote signal nodes on high-interference assembly floors to automate machine health and analytics.

STANDARDS: Modbus/TCP, CANopen, Profinet

Smart Agriculture

We design sealed, solar-powered environmental nodes tracking climate, moisture, and water flow via LoRaWAN radios for large farms and irrigation channels.

STANDARDS: IP67 Weatherproof Enclosures

Wearables & Smart Devices

High-density rigid-flex layouts packed with low-power Bluetooth SoC processors, fine-pitch BGA packaging, and micro energy management systems.

STANDARDS: FCC Part 15, CE RED Certifications
