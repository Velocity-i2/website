[CASE STUDY #01 // AUTOMOTIVE]

Multi-Cell EV Battery Management System

Designing a highly compact, dual-MCU battery management system for utility fleet electric vehicles, managing accurate state-of-charge measurements and passive balancing.

Challenge: High electromagnetic noise interference from electric drivetrains causing signal degradation on voltage measurement rails.
Solution: 12-layer rigid PCB layout with dedicated isolated ground partitions, custom RC input filtering, and isolated SPI communication paths.
Layers: 12 Layers FR4 STATUS: IN MASS PRODUCTION
[CASE STUDY #02 // HEALTHCARE]

ISO 13485 Patient Telemetry Card

Developing a wearable clinical-grade cardiac sensor node communicating bio-metrics over low-latency secure Bluetooth to clinical gateway networks.

Challenge: Extreme space limits requiring analog components and digital BLE SoC to sit inside a tiny 30mm diameter circular profile.
Solution: 6-layer high-density interconnect (HDI) rigid-flex stackup with 0.4mm BGA routing and integrated ceramic antenna.
Layers: 6 Layers HDI Flex STATUS: FDA CERTIFIED
[CASE STUDY #03 // SMART CITY]

Long-Range Municipal Smart City Hub

Designing a multi-channel cellular and LoRaWAN smart lighting controller capable of running indefinitely under absolute high temperature and dust environments.

Challenge: Outdoor operating temperatures soaring up to 55°C, demanding strict component thermal dissipation.
Solution: Copper thermal vias linked directly to an aluminum enclosure heatsink, with high-temp rated AEC component configurations.
Layers: 8 Layers Heavy Copper STATUS: 45,000 UNITS DEPLOYED
[CASE STUDY #04 // AGRI-TECH]

Solar-Powered Rural Irrigation Node

Laying out an ultra-low power sensor terminal reading agricultural soil moisture, solar irradiance, and valve telemetry, transmitting over 15 kilometers.

Challenge: High electrical lightning surges on soil probe cable lines blowing out peripheral input pins.
Solution: Advanced TVS diode arrays, gas discharge tubes, and magnetic isolation on analog signal lines.
Layers: 4 Layers Cost-optimized STATUS: STABLE DEPLOYMENT
