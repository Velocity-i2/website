Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: JUNE 28, 2026

1. Personal Information Collection

We only collect personal information such as name, corporate email address, and company name when you actively submit our contact, role-application, or datasheet request forms. We never trade or lease this information to third parties.

2. Intellectual Property & Schema Security

At Velocity, we understand that your hardware designs represent critical corporate intellectual property. All schematic sheet uploads, mechanical constraints, and firmware requirements are protected by default under strict physical and cloud-encrypted firewalls. We sign mutual Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) prior to auditing or storing any specific hardware design files.

3. Cookies & Telemetry

Our website utilizes local transient sessions and minor telemetry tools to optimize page loading times and trace user navigation flow. It does not load external commercial ad-trackers.

4. Contact Legal Unit

For inquiries regarding data erasure, NDA filing, or security audits of our Vadodara headquarters, please email legal@velocityi2.com.
